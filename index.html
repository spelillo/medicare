Why's my personal information needed?
We use your personal information to set up your secure account on Medicare.gov and give you personalized Medicare information. By creating your account you're agreeing to our privacy policy.
How's my personal information used?
1851(d) of the Social Security Act (42 U.S.C. 1395w-21(d)) lets us give you information and coverage options on Medicare.gov. Certain laws also allow us to use or release your information. Details about the use of your information are in the System of Records Notice "1-800 Medicare Choice (HELPLINE)," System No. 09-70-0535.
